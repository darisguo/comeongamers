GET YOKED: Extreme Bodybuilding

A roguelike deckbuilder game about becoming an ABSOLUTE UNIT!

Game Overview

GET YOKED is an innovative roguelike deckbuilder game that puts you in the shoes of an aspiring bodybuilder. Your goal? To train hard, build muscle, and become the ultimate ABSOLUTE UNIT in the competitive world of bodybuilding.

With strategic card-based gameplay, you'll need to carefully manage your training regimen, nutrition, and recovery to sculpt the perfect physique and dominate competitions.

Key Features

  • Roguelike progression with unique runs each time
  • Strategic deckbuilding mechanics for training
  • Grow and sculpt your body to win competitions
  • Multiple training styles and bodybuilding approaches
  • Challenge yourself to reach the highest level of physical perfection

How To Play

  1. Select cards to build your training deck
  2. Balance workout intensity with proper recovery
  3. Compete in bodybuilding contests to earn rewards
  4. Unlock new exercises and nutrition strategies
  5. Aim to become the ultimate bodybuilding champion

Developer Notes

GET YOKED was developed by Greg's Games and has received positive feedback for its unique combination of roguelike elements and bodybuilding theme. The game offers different versions, with the Windows version featuring enhanced graphics including shadows and improved model rendering.

This browser version allows you to enjoy the core gameplay experience directly online without any downloads required. Jump in and start your journey to becoming the most yoked bodybuilder in the world!
